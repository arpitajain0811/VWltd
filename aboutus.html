ABOUT US

The Vegan Wardrobe is the ultimate destination for sustainable fashion. It is cruelty-free fashion, beauty and lifestyle blog that curates an environment- friendly as well as ethical lifestyle. It was started in August 2016 by us - Two sisters who share a common love for nature and fashion resulting in this green space that nurtures harmless living. It aims to bring a fashion revolution so that fashion can exist responsibly without hurting animals and exploiting humans.
